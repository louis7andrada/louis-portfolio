Who am I ?

I am a Brazilian painter based in Toronto.

I never knew how to express myself the way others seemed to.

I felt alone even when people were around, carried things I could not name and could not release.

I did not understand until much later that painting is the only place I can express and give form to my thoughts.

Most of my work portrays darkness, grief, fear and sadness because that is what I kept enclosed inside my head all those years.

What goes onto the canvas is everything that wished it could have been said, and never was.

Horror, folklore and occult imagery are not escapes for me,

rather they are the inspiration that fuels me to understand my own fears.

Merging my fascination for the dark with my foundation in analog film photography,

my works are built through layers of acrylic that surface through exposure and slow deterioration.

My images tend to not want to be fully seen.
